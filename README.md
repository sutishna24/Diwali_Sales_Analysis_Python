# Diwali_Sales_Analysis_Python_EDA

This repository contains a comprehensive dataset of Diwali sales transactions, including detailed information on customers, products, purchase amounts, and demographics.
The data covers:

**Customer Details:** Unique IDs, names, age, gender, marital status, and occupation.

**Geographical Information:** State and zone breakdown, showing regional trends.

**Sales Insights:** Order count and purchase amount, allowing revenue analysis per product category (like Furniture, Food, Household Items, Electronics, Beauty, Apparel, Games, Books, Pet Care, Sports, and more).

**Product Categories:** Coverage over major retail categories common during Diwali shopping.

**KEY FEATURES**

Comprehensive Retail Dataset: Includes customer profiles (ID, name, age group, gender, marital status), location specifics (state, zone), occupation, product categories, order quantities, and purchase amounts.

Diverse Product Coverage: Transactions span key retail categories such as Furniture, Food, Clothing/Apparel, Electronics, Games, Beauty, Household Items, Pet Care, Books, and more.

Demographic Segmentation: Data enables targeted analysis by age group, gender, marital status, location (state/zone), and occupation.

**INSIGHTS**

**Customer Segmentation:** The data supports segmentation analysis—identifying groups with high purchase potential during Diwali.

**Product Popularity:** Food, Clothing, and Electronics emerge as the top sold categories, reflecting festival shopping trends.

**State-wise Trends:** High transaction volumes are noted in populous states like Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka.

**Occupation Influence:** Purchases are notably high among professionals in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors.

**OUTCOME**

After detailed analysis, the most likely Diwali shoppers are:
Married women aged 26–35 years from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation.
These women are especially inclined to buy products in the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.
